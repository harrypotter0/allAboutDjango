Donald J. Trump

President Elect of the USA

An American Timeline:

  1. June 14, 1946 Born in Jamaica Queens, New York.
  2. At age 13 enrolled in the New York Military Academy School.
  3. Earned a Bachelor of Science Degree in Economics.
  4. Began his Real Estate career before graduating college.
  5. Became President of his family business in the early 70's.
  6. Built Trump Tower in 1983.
  7. Owned the Miss USA and Miss Universe pageants from 1996 to 2015.
  8. Hosted "The Apprentice" from 2004 to 2015.
  9. 324th wealthiest person in the world according to Forbes magazine.
  10. Time magazine's Person of the Year 2016
  11. Won the Presidential election on November 8, 2016.
